ShiftGate Band™ – Smart wristband for warehouse shifts

A rugged wrist device that measures what happens between clock-in and real work.

  • Detects building entry and starts door→station timing.
  • Logs on-time vs late arrival at assigned stations or zones.
  • Tracks on-station vs off-station vs break time.
  • One-tap incident button for hazards, slowdowns or damage.

SafeVision Guard Specs™ – Safety eyewear with incident capture

Safety-certified glasses that protect workers’ eyes and capture short video clips around impacts or flagged events, giving a first-person view of what actually happened on the floor.

  • Impact- or button-triggered short clips (before & after events).
  • Incident-only mode by default for easier policy & privacy alignment.
  • Clips upload securely via dock to Barrow Ops Cloud.
